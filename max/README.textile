h1(#midi-to-csv). The MIDImorphosis MAX/MSP - Max for Live decoder

This patch receives a heart rate signal over MIDI, converts it to an appropriate range and calculates the Inter-Beat Interval (IBI) and heart rate in Beats Per Minute (BPM).
